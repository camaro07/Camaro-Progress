Why I Love Billiard

Billiards isn’t just about hitting balls it's about planning shots, angles, and positioning. It’s a mix of skill, geometry, and strategy, which makes every game a mental challenge.

There’s a certain cool factor to playing pool—the focus, the precision, the sound of a perfect shot. It’s a gamewith history and style.
